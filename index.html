Hello! Welcome to this gesture demo. Until you do something in the gestutre area, the grey box, the bottom text will display "No gesture."

If you click down, it will display "The mouse is down." If you release the mouse click without moving, it will display, "The mouse is up." Moving the mouse to the left or the right while holding the mouse click down will result in text that indicates you either swiped left or right when you release the mouse click.
